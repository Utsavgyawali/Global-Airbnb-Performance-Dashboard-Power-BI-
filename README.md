# Global-Airbnb-Performance-Dashboard-Power-BI-

## Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard (Power BI)

**Tool:** Power BI Desktop | **Data:** Inside Airbnb (10 global cities) | **Pages:** 3



### Overview

This dashboard analyzes Airbnb's global listing data across 10 major cities — Paris, New York, Sydney, Rome, Rio de Janeiro, Istanbul, Mexico City, Bangkok, Cape Town, and Hong Kong  covering approximately 280K listings, 182K unique hosts, and 5.3 million reviews.



### Page 1 — New Listings & Growth Lifecycle

Tracks how Airbnb's new listing count evolved from 2008 to 2021, broken into four room types: Entire Place, Private Room, Hotel Room, and Shared Room.

The chart is annotated with six business lifecycle phases:

- **Introduction (pre-2010):** Slow takeoff with very few listings.
- **Growth (2010–2012):** Rapid expansion begins, primarily Entire Place and Private Room.
- **Maturity (2012–2015):** Listings peak in 2015 — the highest point in Airbnb's history.
- **Decline (2015–2017):** New listings slow due to tightening local regulations globally. Despite this, Airbnb became profitable in H2 2016, with 2017 being its first fully profitable year.
- **Reinvention (2018–2019):** Growth resumes, with a notable increase in Hotel Room listings as Airbnb expanded into traditional hospitality.
- **COVID-19 (2020–2021):** Listings collapse sharply due to global travel restrictions.

**Key Insight:** The ability to turn profitable during a regulatory decline phase demonstrates strong unit economics and operational efficiency.


### Page 2 — Market Share, Pricing & Ratings

**Market Share:**
Paris, New York, and Sydney together account for roughly half of all listings and 59% of total reviews. Paris leads both categories — likely because Airbnb prices are approximately half the cost of Parisian hotel rooms, making it a strong value proposition for guests.

**Average Pricing by Room Type:**
 Room Type                  Avg Price 
 Hotel Room                     $800 
 Entire Place                   $673 
 Shared Room                    $580
 Private Room                   $462

**Ratings (out of 10) across 5 dimensions — Accuracy, Cleanliness, Communication, Location, Value:**
- Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro are the best-rated cities overall.
- Hong Kong and Istanbul consistently score the lowest.
- Cleanliness and Value for money are the two weakest dimensions across all cities, representing the biggest gap for hosts to close.



### Page 3 — Review Frequency, Seasonality & Host Trust

**Review Frequency (used as a booking proxy):**
- 86.5% of customers wrote only one review — indicating most guests are one-time or infrequent visitors.
- 98.8% of customers reviewed 3 times or fewer.
- One outlier wrote 283 reviews, flagged as a potential data anomaly.

**Seasonality:**
- Paris and Rome dominate monthly review share from April to August, reflecting European summer travel peaks.
- New York's share increases in November–December during the holiday season.

**Host Trust Signals:**
- 66.9% of hosts are fully verified with a profile photo — the most trusted category.
- 32.6% have a profile photo but are not identity-verified.
- Only 0.3% are completely anonymous, showing Airbnb's trust systems are highly effective.
